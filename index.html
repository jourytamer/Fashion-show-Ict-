Sustainable Fashion Show

Define the Topic

A sustainable fashion show promotes eco-friendly clothing by showcasing designs made from recycled, organic, or ethically sourced materials. It highlights sustainable production methods, such as zero-waste techniques and fair labor practices, encouraging responsible consumer choices. The goal is to raise awareness about environmental impacts in the fashion industry and inspire a shift toward more sustainable and ethical fashion.

How It's Related to ICT

A sustainable fashion show is related to ICT (Information and Communication Technology) in several ways. ICT helps in designing eco-friendly clothing using digital tools like CAD software, reducing material waste. It also enables virtual fashion shows, minimizing carbon footprints from travel and production. Additionally, ICT supports online marketing, e-commerce, and blockchain for transparent supply chains, promoting ethical sourcing and sustainability in the fashion industry.
